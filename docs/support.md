# Pop Kollection Support

Welcome to Pop Kollection support! This page provides help and answers to common questions about using the app.

## Getting Started

Pop Kollection is a simple, offline pop vinyl figure collection tracker for iOS. All your data stays on your device‚Äîno account required!

### First Steps

1. **Add Your First Item**

   - Tap the "Add" tab (‚ûï icon)
   - Fill in the details (name, series, number, etc.)
   - Add photos from your camera or photo library
   - Tap "Save"

2. **Browse Your Collection**

   - Tap the "Home" tab to see all your items
   - Use the search bar to find specific items
   - Tap any card to view details

3. **Edit or Delete Items**
   - Long press on any card
   - Select "Edit" or "Delete" from the menu
   - Changes are saved instantly

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add multiple photos?

1. When adding/editing an item, tap "Add Photos"
2. Choose "Take Photo" or "Choose from Library"
3. Select up to 10 photos per item
4. Photos are stored locally on your device

### Is my data backed up?

No, all data is stored locally on your device. We recommend:

- Regular iOS device backups via iCloud or iTunes
- Exporting data periodically (feature coming soon)

### What happens if I uninstall the app?

All your data will be deleted. Make sure to back up your device before uninstalling.

### Does the app require internet?

No! Pop Kollection works completely offline. No internet connection required.

### Can I sync across devices?

Not yet. Currently, data stays on one device. Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

### How do I delete an item?

1. Long press on the card
2. Tap "Delete"
3. Confirm deletion

### How do I search my collection?

1. Go to the "Home" or "Search" tab
2. Type in the search bar
3. Search works on name, series, number, category, and notes

### How do I share my collection?

Currently, you can share individual item cards using the share button (coming soon). Full collection export/sharing is planned for future updates.

### Can I import data from a spreadsheet?

CSV import is planned for a future premium update. Stay tuned!

### Is there an Android version?

Not yet. iOS-only for now. Android support is planned for future releases.

## Troubleshooting

### Photos not saving

- **Check Permissions:** Settings ‚Üí Pop Kollection ‚Üí Photos ‚Üí Allow Access
- **Storage Full:** Free up space on your device
- **Restart App:** Close and reopen the app

### App crashes or freezes

- **Update App:** Make sure you have the latest version
- **Restart Device:** Sometimes a simple restart helps
- **Reinstall App:** Delete and reinstall (warning: you'll lose data)

### Search not working

- **Check Spelling:** Make sure search terms are correct
- **Try Different Terms:** Search by name, series, or number
- **Clear Search:** Tap the X to reset

### Can't add photos

- **Check Permissions:** Settings ‚Üí Pop Kollection ‚Üí Camera/Photos
- **Photo Limit:** Max 10 photos per item
- **File Size:** Very large images may fail (try smaller photos)

## Tips & Best Practices

### Organizing Your Collection

- Use consistent naming (e.g., "Spider-Man" not "Spiderman")
- Include series names for easy searching
- Add purchase dates to track when you got items
- Take clear photos of front and back

### Tracking Value

- Update current values periodically
- Use price guides like Pop Price Guide
- Track condition changes over time
- Note any box damage in the notes field

### Photo Tips

- Take photos in good lighting
- Include box condition details
- Capture any special variants or chase stickers
- Keep photos under 5MB for best performance
